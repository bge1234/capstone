Note: This is a mobile app. The best way to view it is to use Google Chrome's dev tools (hamburger icon > More Tools > Developer Tools) and then click on the two rectangles icon to the left of "Elements". This will force the app to display approximately as it would on a phone, save for a few things which are unique to phones.

Here is the actual homepage of the app: https://moveit-benebel.firebaseapp.com/www/index.html

Here the internal API, which takes several other APIs and some manually-entered database contents, and turns them into one JSON: https://moveitapi-benebel.herokuapp.com



At first, you will see the blank app. The star or sliding the background to the right will show saved matches once you have them. The eye will shows your preferences. The notepad will allow you to edit your preferences.

Fill out your preferences after clicking on the notebad, and then click the Save button at the bottom. You can always return later to complete or alter your preferences.

You can view your preferences without editing them by clicking on the eye.

After saving your preferences, your matches will be visible on the main screen.

Swipe left to save a match.

Swipe right to delete a match.

Your saved matches can be viewed by clicking on the star or sliding the background to the right.
